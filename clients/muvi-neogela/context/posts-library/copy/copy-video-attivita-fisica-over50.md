## 🎬 NOTE PER VIDEOGRAFO

**Visual Tone:**
- Real, authentic, not polished
- Diverse ages (45, 55, 65 years old)
- Diverse body types
- Natural lighting when possible
- Domestic/relatable settings

**Key Moments to Capture:**
- Woman hesitating before movement → then doing it
- Different activities (camminata, pesi, pilates, ballo)
- Close-up of face showing joy/relief
- Before/after posture comparison
- Final CTA moment (looking at camera, smile)

**Music/Sound:**
- Upbeat but not cheesy
- Empowering tone
- International appeal (no lyrics in Italian if selling broader)
- Trending audio for TikTok

**NO-NOs:**
- Don't show extreme fitness (intimidating)
- Don't use super young people (breaks trust)
- Don't make it look too easy/unrealistic
- Don't focus on weight loss (tangential to message)
